Courier Services for Manufacturing

The East Midlands has a proud manufacturing heritage, and Roebuck Courier Service is proud to support the businesses that keep it going. From aerospace and engineering to food production and logistics, we provide the dedicated delivery services that manufacturers depend on.

When a production line is waiting on a component, or a customer is waiting on an order, every hour matters. Our same-day courier service gets your materials, parts, and finished goods where they need to be — quickly, safely, and on a dedicated vehicle.

With our XLWB vans capable of carrying up to 4 standard pallets with a 1,300kg payload, we can handle consignments that would require a separate haulage booking through traditional networks.

What We Move for Manufacturers

Raw Materials & Components

Urgent deliveries of materials needed to keep production running on schedule.

Finished Goods

Dedicated delivery of finished products to customers, distributors, or retail partners.

Production Parts

JIT (just-in-time) parts delivery to keep assembly lines moving.

Samples & Prototypes

Careful handling of prototypes, samples, and test pieces between factories and clients.

Pallet Deliveries

Up to 4 standard pallets in our XLWB vans — no need for separate haulage.

Inter-Site Transfers

Regular runs between manufacturing sites, warehouses, and distribution centres.
